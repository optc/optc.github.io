Instructions
How to use this Calculator:
  1. Select the Unit you want to Skill up/Socket
  2. Enter whatever Sockets you already have on that Unit
    • Click on a Socket to change its level
  3. Enter the Amount of Books/Duplicates you have of that Unit
  4. Enter the Amount of Skill Ups you need
    • If you want to calculate the chance during a Special Level Chance Up Event,
      check the box for it
  5. Click Calculate for whichever Number you want to calculate
  6. Go and cry in a corner because the chance is way too low
