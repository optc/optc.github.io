Instructions
Hold down the desired action while clicking in order to change how the action works.

Damage Window: (Right side)

  • Middle Mouse Click: Changes Miss > Below Good > Good > Great > Perfect
  • CTRL: Changes Miss > Below Good > Good > Great > Perfect
  • SHIFT: Changes Opponent's Type (For individual targets)
  • Drag & Drop: Changes Attack Order

Units Window: (Center area)
  • Middle Mouse Click: Changes Miss > Below Good > Good > Great > Perfect
  • CTRL: Changes Orbs Matching > Opposite > None (You may also click on orb)
  • SHIFT: Despairs Captain
  • ALT: Locks Units
  • SHIFT + ALT: Disables Unit
  • Drag & Drop: Change Unit Position
  • Click on Level: Opens level slider
  • Middle Mouse Click on Level: Sets unit to MAX level

Ship Slider: (Under Settings)
  • CTRL: Opens Ship Selection Window

Enemy Defense: (Under Settings)
  • CTRL: Opens Defense Selection Window

Stat Window: (Under Units Window)
  • Drag Slider: Adjusts Health Percentage
  • Left Click on Health: Opens prompt where you can set HP or HP in %
