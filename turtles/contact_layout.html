No.

For the 24-hour event and fortnight isles, you can see the order here. As for everything else(Sugofest, colored turtles, etc.), I find out the same time you do via the in-game news.

Eventually, yes. But it's low on the priority list since I have no experience developing apps.

My time zone is incorrect.

The library I'm using to detect the time zone has a bug where it doesn't detect some places properly such as NZ/Auckland. However, the calculator will still be correct because it doesn't rely on your time zone, but the current time on your computer.
